Program Name

GroupMe Calendar Monitor SMS Notifications

Program Description

GroupMe Calendar Monitor is a private, family-use application that automatically detects scheduled events (games, practices, tournaments, etc.) from GroupMe group chats and syncs them to Google Calendar. SMS notifications are sent to inform family members when calendar events are created, updated, or cancelled.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. The system checks for updates every 30 minutes and sends a single batched SMS per cycle only when calendar changes are detected. Typical volume is 0-5 messages per day depending on sports activity. No recurring or marketing messages are sent.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Opt-In

By configuring your phone number in the application, you consent to receive automated SMS notifications about calendar updates from GroupMe group chats.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of receiving messages at any time:

  • Text STOP to the sending number to immediately stop all messages.
  • Contact the application administrator to have your number removed from the configuration.

Help

For support or questions about this messaging program:

  • Text HELP to the sending number for assistance.
  • Contact the application administrator directly.

Supported Carriers
